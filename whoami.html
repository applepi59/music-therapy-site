About Me

My name is Zinor Ayanat, and I am a student at Nazarbayev Intellectual School of Chemistry and Biology in Atyrau. This project was born from a very personal and relatable place — I, like many of my classmates, experience high levels of academic stress on a daily basis. I wanted to explore a solution that felt both accessible and meaningful, not just for me, but for others facing the same struggle.

Music has always been my way to feel safe, grounded, and calm. It inspired me to research whether it could be more than a personal comfort — could it actually be a tool to fight academic stress? My project investigates the effectiveness of music therapy among high school and university students in Kazakhstan, both from a scientific and emotional perspective.

“Sometimes I listen to music during study breaks, and it calms my anxiety better than anything else.” — Survey respondent

By combining real data, emotional insight, and a deep love for music, I hope this website and this project can inspire others to turn to music not only for enjoyment, but also for mental balance.
